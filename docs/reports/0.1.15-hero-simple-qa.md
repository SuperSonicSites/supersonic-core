## 4. Findings

### High

| # | Pattern | Finding | Evidence | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| H1 | hero-simple | Group justification still does not satisfy the hero control contract. | The rendered outer group has `is-content-justification-left`, but the inner constrained group still centers its child content at 760px. On desktop, the outer group begins at `x=72`, the inner group at `x=137`, and the H1 starts at `x=340`, so the visible hero copy is centered rather than left-positioned. This confirms the manual editor test that left/center/right still does not visibly work as intended. | Do not put the 760px constraint on a nested constrained group that then centers its own children. Either let the hero section group own `contentSize: "760px"` and `justifyContent`, with a documented exception to the current full-width `contentSize` rule, or use a different native structure where the 760px content wrapper itself has a real width that can be positioned by the parent. |

### Medium

| # | Pattern | Finding | Evidence | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| M1 | hero-simple | Placeholder button links remain. | Both CTA buttons still use `href="#"`. | Wire real destinations before launch or keep this tracked as starter-content debt. |

## 7. Considered And Dismissed

| Item | Why dismissed |
| --- | --- |
| Mobile rendering as a blocking layout defect | Mobile is readable, buttons are visible, and no overflow was observed. The confirmed blocker is editor-control behavior, not mobile layout. |

---

## 8. Remaining Risks / Next Fix

- The 0.1.15 fix did not solve the Gutenberg justification problem.
- A follow-up source change is needed before another release.
- The likely architectural decision is whether to relax the "no `contentSize` on full-width section group" rule for hero patterns, because native Gutenberg constrained-layout justification appears to expect the selected group itself to own the content width.
- After the fix, re-run the same one-pattern QA flow and manually test the block editor controls again.

---

## 9. Approval Status

**FAIL** - `Hero: Simple` is not approved in 0.1.15. The release is active on staging, but the intended editor-control fix does not work.
