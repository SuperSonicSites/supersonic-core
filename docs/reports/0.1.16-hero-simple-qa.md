| # | Pattern | Finding | Evidence | Fix |

| H1 | hero-simple | The pattern fix works locally, but the page template still double-applies the horizontal gutter on rendered pages. | On desktop, `main` applied 72px left/right padding, the `alignfull` hero remained inside the 1296px post-content area, and the hero group then applied another 5% internal padding.
